About Sam

Sam WS Choi is a recording, mixing, mastering, and audio post-production engineer.

Having been raised in Seoul, South Korea, he was naturally exposed to music from a very young age. He began learning instruments such as piano, violin, trumpet in elementary school. He taught himself how to play guitar and sing when he moved to Canada to attend middle school and high school. Through active participation in the school music program, Sam began his journey of studying music.

It was not until Sam started his studies at Berklee College of Music that his career as an audio engineer began. After getting accepted to the Music Production and Engineering program, Sam quickly combined his visual media interests with audio engineering interests. Sam is currently completing his major in Music Production and Engineering with a minor in Audio Post-production while actively working as a freelance engineer, taking on various projects.
